About Us

We Help Our Members To Grow Their Business

Rachuonyo East Entrepreneurs Savings and credit Cooperative Society Ltd (REES SACCO) was Registered in September 2018. This organization was established by entrepreneurs of Rachuonyo East sub county with the main objective of organizing and promoting the welfare and economic interest of its members.

To promote a saving culture, provide diverse, competitive and affordable financial services to socially and economically empower our members.

To be a leading SACCO in Kenya.

Core Values

Self-help promotes individual empowerment, while mutual responsibility fosters collective accountability. Equality ensures fairness, equity promotes justice and inclusivity. Honesty, openness, and social responsibility guide transparent, community-oriented actions integral to all sacco activities.

Co-operative Principles

The cooperative prioritizes democratic principles for equitable member participation and autonomy in decision-making. Education empowers members, while cooperation fosters common goals. Community welfare is central, ensuring the well-being of all involved.

Membership

Membership requires payment of the registration fee, attainment of minimum shares, and consistent monthly savings. It can be as part of a registered social group recognized by the Kenyan government, as registered individuals within a group, or as an unaffiliated individual member

Why Choose Us!

Why People Choose Us!

REES aims at enhancing the Economic and Social welfare of its members. There are very few established financial institutions that provide services to entrepreneurs in the informal sector, which are the main target of REES. The primary targets of REES include, but are not limited to, subsistence farmers, small-scale traders (market vendors) with a focus on women, widows, widowers, youths, vulnerable groups, and persons with disabilities (PWDs) who are not in formal employment.
